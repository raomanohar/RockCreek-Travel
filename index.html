Rockcreek Travel

A division of Cerner Corporation

For 40 years, Cerner has worked at the intersection of health care and information technology to connect people and system around the world at more than 27,000 facilities in over 35 countries. As Cerner’s travel agency since 2011, Rockcreek Travel works to facilitate the travel process for Cerner associates as they travel to establish, support and maintain important client relationships across the globe.

Mission Statement: “To create an efficient and effective managed travel process, that provides for the safety and comfort of our traveling associates, at a reasonable cost to Cerner and our clients.”

Hotels

Our global hotel program supports our traveling associates by negotiating annual rates and coordinating room blocks at clean, safe hotels with easy access to our clients. If you would like to inquire about adding your hotel to our program, please contact us at hotels@rockcreektravel.com.

Ground Transportation

Our associates utilize a wide range of transportation modes to maximize their time spent with clients. If you would like to inquire about how you can join our travel program, please contact us at groundtransportation@rockcreektravel.com.
